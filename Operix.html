Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No! 14-day free trial, no credit card required. After trial, choose if you want to continue.

How's the setup? Is it difficult?

Super easy! Just 5 minutes. Add products, add users, and you're ready. We have tutorial videos and free onboarding support.

Can it work offline? What if there's no internet?

We recommend stable internet for best performance. All data is cloud-based and accessible from any device with internet connection.

How many users can I have?

Monthly plan: up to 10 users. Annual plan: up to 20 users. Need more? We have an Enterprise plan with unlimited users.

Included! Automated email notifications for orders, deliveries, and payment reminders. No extra charges.

100% secure! Everything is encrypted. Daily backups. Hosted on Firebase (Google infrastructure). Enterprise-grade security.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Export to Excel (CSV) or PDF anytime. Your data, your control. No lock-in.

Do you have customer support?

Yes! Email & chat support for all plans. Tagalog or English. Average response time: 2-4 hours on business days.
